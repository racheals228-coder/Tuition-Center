About Raquel Tuition Centre

Empowering students through quality education, personalized learning, and academic excellence in Mathematics and Computer Studies.

Who We Are

Raquel Tuition Centre is a learning institution committed to helping students achieve academic excellence through quality education, personalized support, and effective teaching methods. We specialize in Mathematics and Computer Studies, providing students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to succeed in their academic journey and future careers.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality educational support that empowers students to achieve academic excellence, develop critical thinking skills, and become lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be a leading tuition centre recognized for academic excellence, innovation in teaching, and positive student outcomes.

Our Core Values

📖 Excellence

We strive to deliver high-quality education and encourage students to achieve their full academic potential.

🤝 Integrity

We promote honesty, responsibility, and ethical behavior in all learning activities.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methods and creative approaches to enhance learning experiences.

Student Success

We are committed to supporting every student in achieving academic excellence and personal growth.

Meet the Founder

Racheal Sakala
